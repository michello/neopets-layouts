about

What's a better way to end and start the year than to reflect on the anime that has either inspired or ruined us the past year?

Welcome to Snapshots, a page and activity in which we'll reminisce and review anime that have came out in the past year and rank them collectively for what's the best or worst anime, creating snapshots that we can look fondly back when time comes.

how it works

  1. We've already selected several candidates for best anime in each season of 2018 and for worst anime in each season of 2018.
  2. During AMW (February 2nd & February 9th), we'll watch one or two episodes of each candidate (depending on whether we can get through the anime) and alternate between a best and worst anime candidate.
  3. After AMW, we ask that you neomail Rebecca about the anime we've watched and the rating you give the anime. If you have any commentary of the show you watched please include it in the neomail as well!
  4. if you are not able to attend AMW, fear not! We'll be posting the list of candidates we watch/cover here so you're more than welcome to watch the first two episodes of whichever candidates you prefer and submit your feedback normally then. We hope you have fun!

Rating system will work as follows:

  • For best anime candidate:
    1 ("This is actually terrible.") → 10 ("This anime saved humanity.")
  • For worst anime candidate:
    1 ("This show is actually tolerable!") → 10 ("This show makes me want to disappear.")
