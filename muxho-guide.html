welcome to the crazy

Hi there! If you're reading this, you're either new, reacquainting with our policies, or stalking us (and if you're the latter, that's cool. We dig every type of deres, including the yanderes. *wink*). Anyways, welcome to MuxHo!

We're a chill, chat based guild with a pretense of being anime/manga themed for the sake of seeming like a legitimate guild.

messages

You may have read the rules on our webbie but we can't help but stress our policies with posting on the message board enough:

  1. Please don't post more than three times in a row!
  2. Use a legible font. Please make sure your text font, or neoHTML, is colored black or another dark shade so it's legible!
  3. Add your name to your signature or userlookup! We prefer to have your name in your signature since it's a hassle having to go through the pesky captchas to view your lookup.
  4. If there's an existing conversation, DON'T be afraid to jump right into it. :* Or you can also start your own conversation. We're chatterboxes. We don't mind!

participation policy

We understand if real life gets in the way but MuxHo is also not a place to let your accounts clutter. This may be new to older members but PLEASE make an effort to post at least once a week and ten times in a month. Otherwise you may be in jeopardy of getting swept from the guild.

If you do have any emergency that prevents you from making the required amount, do neomail one of the council members ahead of time!

muxho life & culture

We're a pretty chill crowd so don't worry about having to think of every weeb content you can include in a comment! More often than not, our conversations circles around our everyday life so don't be afraid to be yourself. If you're comfortable, be sure to join our FB group, which is of the same name as our guild (Mugen no Hoshi), to be updated on when AMW is.

That said, we do highly recommend participating in AMW, or anime movie weekends. It's a one day event that is usually held on Saturdays, or whenever the council members decides to have it. It's a great way to bond with other members in an unconstrained environment. For more information of the event, click here.

We also have activities occasionally to celebrate the season, holiday, etc. Feel free to glance at our activities page to see what they're like.

the tech

And where would we be in life without technology? As you know, Neopets has a really terrible user experience, with many problems unaddressed. To combat and make the user experience better, we've come up with technological solutions!

Feel free to neomail michelle if you have a software idea for the guild that you want to be made!

neoify || github @ ml4963/neoify

We all know it can be a pain to wait for someone to post on the message board. Fear not! If you use google chrome, you can get our chrome extension that both alerts you when someone makes a post and tells you whether your typed out message will bypass filters or not.

If you are also interested in helping out with bumping the guild ad board, this extension will also alert you when the ad board falls below too low in the GC.

neotracker || github @ ml4963/neotracker

This app is more for council members but as a member, it can be confusing to keep track the number of posts you've made over the couple weeks and make sure you're not in danger of getting swept.

NeoTracker not only keeps track of how many posts you've made on a daily basis, it also shows how many posts the guild has made!

Check when guild is most busy to get in on the fun or when it's least, to help stir up conversations.
