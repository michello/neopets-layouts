Welcome

Mugen no Hoshi literally translates to "star of infinity", but we took liberties to define it as "infinite number of stars." It refers to how much we have to deal with on an everyday basis, which can be rough. We understand!

Thus, we hope for Mugen no Hoshi to offer as much escapism as anime or manga does!

Though we are anime/manga themed, we are chat based at our core. A lot of our conversations end up revolving around random topics such as discussing our favorite Christmas shows in the middle of January or lamenting over the recent advent of horribly soap opera-y teen drama adaptations.

Still unsure what it means to be in MuxHo? Take a look at what we've done before or what makes us awesome.

  • Had a very extensive conversation over what one of our members should get at Taco Bell.

...and more!

We're always open to suggestions for what to do next! And the only way to find out what our next adventure is is to join us. ;)
