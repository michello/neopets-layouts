welcome

Mugen no Hoshi literally translates to "star of infinity", but we took liberties to define it as "infinite number of stars." It refers to how much we have to deal with on an everyday basis, which can be rough. We understand!

Thus, we hope for Mugen no Hoshi to offer as much escapism as anime or manga does!

Though we are anime/manga/gaming themed, we are chat based at our core. A lot of our conversations revolves around random topics such as discussing our favorite Christmas shows in the middle of January or lamenting over the recent advent of horribly soap opera-y teen drama adaptations.

Still unsure what it means to be in MuxHo? Take a look at what we've done before or what makes us awesome.
