Guidelines

  1. Recreate a character that follows the theme for this month with Dress to Impress.
  2. Send the picture of your entry to Planet either with a link or the picture on a petpage. We'll be displaying all of the entries below.
  3. During the last week of the month we'll have anonymous voting where a poll will be created in the guild and members can vote on which entry they prefer the best!
  4. The entry with the most votes will recieve a small prize! Best of luck!
